Corned Beef Stew Recipe

Corned Beef Stew

Corned Beef Stew is an african dish.It is a tomato based stew and is simple and easy to make, as long as you have the right ingredients.

The dish can take a couple hours to make, however, it is a dish that can be eaten over multiple weeks, if you freeze the stew after eating.

Ingredients

  • Stewed Tomatos
  • Canned Corned Beef
  • Onions
  • Ginger
  • Oilve Oil
  • Garlic Salt
  • Curry Powder

Steps

  1. Blend stewed tomato, onion, and ginger together
  2. Add the bleneded vegetables to a pot
  3. Leave pot on the stove to boil
  4. In a separate pot, add three cans of corned beef
  5. Add corned beef to tomato pot
  6. Add garlic salt and curry powder to stew
  7. Let the the stew sit for five minutes, then serve
