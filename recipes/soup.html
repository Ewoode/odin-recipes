Tomato Soup Recipe

Tomato Soup

Tomato soup is an easy and delicious recipe. It can be eaten when you're sick or just for your pleasure!

This dish can be made in as quickly as five minutes, so it's good for a quick lunch on the go.

Ingredients

  • Fresh chopped tomatos
  • Sliced onion
  • Chicken broth
  • Cloves
  • Butter
  • All purpose flour
  • Salt
  • White sugar

Steps

  1. Combine tomatos, onion,cloves, and chicken broth to a stockpot
  2. Bring the pot to a boil for twenty minutes
  3. In an empty stockpot, melt the butter over medium heat
  4. Wisk tomato into mixture so no lumps form, then stir the rest
  5. Season with salt and sugar, adjust the taste to your enjoyment
